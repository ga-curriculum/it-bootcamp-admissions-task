## Goal

The goal of the admissions assessment for the IT Bootcamp is to create a Google Doc and share it with your admissions advisor so they are able to view it.

## Steps

1. Create a new Google Doc.

2. Rename the document to `IT Admissions Assessment - Your Name`, replacing `Your Name` with your full name.

3. Publish the document so anyone on the internet can view it.

4. Share the URL with your admissions advisor.
